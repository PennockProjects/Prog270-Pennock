Android Customizations. Feature 01.

This page was added to the Android Settings by Asus, the company that makes this tablet. Air Droid a great way to control your device

With Air Droid you can access your Android device from your laptop. Once you're logged in, you can access your contacts, apps, and all sorts of other information on your phone. Read more or download AirDroid for Android at CNET Download.com
