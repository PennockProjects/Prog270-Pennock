Contact Theseus

We would adivse against contacting Theseus. He is known for causing trouble for those that contact him.Theseus, when he left to slay the Minotaur on Crete, promised to return with white sails if he survived so that his worried father would know he was alive. Unfortunately, he 'forgot' the promise and sailed back to Athens with black sails. His father, King Aegeus, threw himself into the sea in grief before seeing his victorious son arrive. The sea is called the Aegean Sea after that.
